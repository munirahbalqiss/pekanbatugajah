Programs & Activities

Our array of community programs and activities are designed to bring residents together, fostering a vibrant and connected neighborhood here in Pekan Batu Gajah. From educational workshops to lively cultural events, we offer a diverse range of opportunities for every participation in building a stronger sense of belonging among community.

Previous & Upcoming Programs and Activities

PROGRAM TANGGUNGJAWAB SOSIAL KORPORAT

23 January 2024 - A corporate social responsibility (CSR) program with PEISB regarding the issue of drains, garbage and bush poaching took place behind the Yong Fong coffee shop and the Pusing movie theater.

MAJLIS PELANCARAN BUKU MURALISM

22 January - The Batu Gajah District Council has received an invitation for the Muralism Book Launch Ceremony related to paintings that have been carried out in collaboration between the Batu Gajah District Council and Mara University of Technology (Seri Iskandar branch).

MyBG SENAM DAN SIHAT

22 December 2023 - The MyBG Exercise and Health program for civil servants was held on 22 December 2023 at 8.00 am. The program started with a brisk walk for a distance of 3.0 km and herb tree planting activities at Changkat Public Park. The activity ends with light exercise. Hopefully this activity can help improve fitness and increase work productivity.

KEMPEN BATU GAJAH BERSIH, HIJAU, INDAH

17 December 2023 - The Batu Gajah District Council has organized an Illegal Garbage Site Purification Program and a Plastic Bag Free Day Campaign. The inauguration of the program was completed by Puan Sandrea Ng Shy Ching, Chairman of the Perak State Housing and Local Government Committee.
