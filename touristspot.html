Silverlake Village Outlet

"Step into the heart of shopping bliss at Silverlake Village Outlet in Batu Gajah. Uncover a world of style, variety, and unbeatable deals in every corner. From trendy boutiques to cozy cafes, this village-style outlet is a haven for fashion enthusiasts, foodies, and bargain hunters alike. Immerse yourself in a unique shopping experience, where every stroll unveils hidden gems and every purchase becomes a delightful discovery. Silverlake Village Outlet: Your gateway to unparalleled shopping delights in the charming landscapes of Batu Gajah."

Rumah Dato Lat

"Step into the enchanting world of Rumah Dato' Lat in Batu Gajah, where the vivid strokes of Malaysia's beloved cartoonist come to life. Immerse yourself in the charm of Datuk Lat's iconic residence, a living gallery celebrating the heart and soul of Malaysian culture. Explore the pages of history through his captivating artworks, and let the spirit of his creativity transport you to a whimsical realm of laughter and nostalgia. A cultural haven awaits at Rumah Dato' Lat – where every frame tells a story, and every visitor becomes a part of the vibrant tapestry of Malaysian heritage."

Kellie's Castle

"Step into the enchanting realms of Kellie's Castle, where history whispers through ancient corridors. Uncover the mysteries of this majestic Perak landmark, where each stone tells a tale of ambition, love, and the timeless echoes of a bygone era. Kellie's Castle: A living testament to the dreams that linger in the heart of Malaysia."

Entrance Fee

Silverlake Village Outlet Rumah Dato Lat Kellies Castle
The entrance fee and parking is free. But, the activities have to be paid individually depends on what games/activities tourists wants to do. Previously in September 2023, the entry was free. However, the ticket price has not been updated as the gallery is under maintenance until March 2024 The entrance fee for foreign tourists is RM10 (adult) and RM8 (child), while Malaysians need to pay RM5 (adult) and RM3 (child) to enter.
